# ERP Accounting Structure
`I recommend a 5-level structure similar to:`
```bash
Assets
├── Current Assets
│   ├── Cash
│   ├── Bank
│   ├── Accounts Receivable
│   ├── Inventory
│
├── Fixed Assets
│   ├── Furniture
│   ├── Vehicle
│   └── Equipment

Liabilities
├── Current Liabilities
│   ├── Accounts Payable
│   ├── Tax Payable

Equity
├── Owner Capital
├── Retained Earnings

Income
├── Sales Revenue
├── Other Income

Expenses
├── Purchase Expense
├── Salary Expense
├── Rent Expense
├── Utility Expense
```
